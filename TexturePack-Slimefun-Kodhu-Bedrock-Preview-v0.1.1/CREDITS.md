## Merge Policy

The pack uses the newest available Slimefun resource pack as the main base, then adds missing files from the other packs. When the same file exists in several sources, the newer 1.21.5+ compatible version is preferred to reduce model and overlay conflicts.

Most textures come from external community packs. Rights remain with their original authors.
